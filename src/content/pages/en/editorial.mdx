## Editorial standards

This template assumes a tutorial site should optimize for:

- clearer onboarding paths
- practical verification steps
- explicit troubleshooting guidance
- stable slugs and durable information architecture

## Update triggers

Downstream sites should update articles when:

- product setup changes materially
- an old guide becomes misleading
- a better canonical workflow exists

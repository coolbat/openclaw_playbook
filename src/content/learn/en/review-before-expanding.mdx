## Expansion is expensive

Every new branch, channel, or task adds complexity. If you expand before you review, you scale confusion along with capability.

## A simple review frame

- what worked repeatedly?
- what failed more than once?
- what still depends on manual rescue?
- what is unclear to another teammate?

## Use review to choose the next move

Good review helps you decide whether to simplify, instrument, or expand. Without review, the next change is often just another guess.

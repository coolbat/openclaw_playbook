## Narrow scope creates momentum

Beginners often think progress means adding more capabilities. In reality, progress usually looks like making one narrow workflow boringly reliable.

## What narrow means

- one owner
- one trigger
- one output surface
- one easy review step

## Why it works

Small workflows teach you faster because every result is legible. You can tell what improved and what still breaks.

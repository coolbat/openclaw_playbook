## When to use this template

Use this when a setup guide must fit Chinese tooling reality instead of assuming a default Western stack. It is useful for onboarding, internal handoff, and localization work.

## Minimal setup

Choose one workflow, one local toolchain, and one audience. The first version should focus on compatibility and clarity rather than full coverage.

## What good output looks like

The brief should explain the path, the key constraints, and the local equivalents of tools or flows that new users may not recognize immediately.

## First extension

Add bilingual glossary terms after the main path is stable. That usually reduces confusion in mixed-language teams.

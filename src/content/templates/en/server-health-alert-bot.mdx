## When to use this template

Use this when your team needs earlier visibility into service degradation. It is especially useful when a human usually notices problems too late.

## Minimal setup

Start with one service, a few clear health indicators, and one alert destination. Early versions should aim for clarity, not alert coverage of every failure mode.

## What good output looks like

A strong alert tells the team what failed, how serious it is, and what context matters first. It should reduce triage time, not increase confusion.

## First extension

Add escalation or summarization rules only after the base alert quality is trustworthy. Otherwise you amplify noise at scale.

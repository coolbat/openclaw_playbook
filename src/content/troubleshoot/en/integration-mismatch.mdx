## What this symptom usually means

Routing mistakes often look more complicated than they are. The workflow may be succeeding, but the destination is misconfigured.

## First recovery loop

Print the current destination config and send one narrow integration test. Confirm exactly where the result is supposed to land.

## What to verify next

Check workspace IDs, channel IDs, webhook targets, and copied settings from other environments. These small mismatches create large confusion.

## If the issue persists

Test the same destination outside the full workflow. That helps isolate routing from generation.

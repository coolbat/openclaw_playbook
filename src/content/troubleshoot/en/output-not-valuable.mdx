## What this symptom usually means

This is often a workflow design problem, not a runtime problem. The system may be healthy while the framing of the task is still too vague.

## First recovery loop

Restate the expected artifact in one sentence. If you cannot describe the output clearly, the workflow probably cannot produce it clearly either.

## What to verify next

Compare the result with a real human baseline and reduce the workflow to a narrower deliverable. You want one useful output before you want a sophisticated system.

## If the issue persists

Revisit the workflow design assumptions. Good automation usually starts with one crisp artifact, not with a fully abstract prompt.

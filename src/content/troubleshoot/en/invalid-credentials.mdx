## What this symptom usually means

The key may exist, but that does not mean it is usable. Scope, quota, region, or endpoint mismatch often explains this kind of failure better than application logic does.

## First recovery loop

Validate one direct provider call outside the full workflow. Prove that the credential works in the simplest possible context first.

## What to verify next

Check quota, account scope, and endpoint precision. Many "valid" credentials fail because they belong to the wrong workspace or endpoint variant.

## If the issue persists

Use a known-good credential from a controlled environment for one test. That isolates provider setup from the rest of the stack.

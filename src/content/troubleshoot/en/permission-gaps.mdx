## What this symptom usually means

This usually indicates a permissions boundary problem rather than an OpenClaw reasoning problem. Read access may exist while write access is missing.

## First recovery loop

Identify the exact action that failed and test the smallest possible version of that write or integration step.

## What to verify next

Compare granted scopes with required scopes and confirm the bot is actually invited into the destination workspace or channel.

## If the issue persists

Replace the integration with a simpler target temporarily. That tells you whether the workflow is blocked by permissions or by a broader logic issue.

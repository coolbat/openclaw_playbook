## What this symptom usually means

Logs are giving you too much raw information without enough context. Status narrows the search space before logs become useful.

## First recovery loop

Identify which subsystem is unhealthy first. Then filter logs around one failure window instead of reading everything at once.

## What to verify next

Write down one concrete hypothesis before the next config change. That makes each log reading session more intentional and less reactive.

## If the issue persists

Return to a shorter health loop and simplify the reproduction again. Better evidence usually beats deeper logging.

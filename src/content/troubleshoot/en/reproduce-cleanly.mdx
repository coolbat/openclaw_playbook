## What this symptom usually means

The failure may be real, but your reproduction method is unstable. Changing too many variables hides the true fault.

## First recovery loop

Choose one channel, one message, one model, and one workflow path. Keep every other variable fixed.

## What to verify next

Capture the exact failing output once and use it as the reference for all follow-up checks. Stable evidence is more valuable than more experiments.

## If the issue persists

Move the reproduction into a minimal environment. The simpler the test, the clearer the diagnosis.
